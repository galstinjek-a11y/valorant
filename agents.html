AGENTS

Agents are the core of Valorant, each bringing unique abilities and playstyles that shape how every match is played. Choosing the right agent isnt just about aim—its about strategy, teamwork, and adapting to the situation.

Since there is around 29 diffrent valorant agents, they are split into 4 diffrent roles, each role having simmilar abilities.

ROLES

Duelists are aggressive entry players who create space for their team. They are built for taking fights and securing early picks.

Sentinels are defensive experts who lock down areas and protect teammates. They excel at holding sites and watching flanks.

Initiators gather information and disrupt enemies. They help the team push into sites by clearing angles and forcing opponents out of position.

Controlers specialize in blocking vision and controlling key areas of the map. Their abilities help teams execute strategies safely.

Play your way! 🎯

With a wide variety of agents available, every player can find a style that fits them—whether its aggressive, tactical, or supportive.
Mastering multiple agents will give you an edge and make you more valuable to your team.
