VALORANT

Maps Roles Weapons

The buy menu in Valorant is where every round begins. Players spend credits to purchase weapons, shields, and abilities, making each decision important for the teams success. Choosing the right loadout depends on your economy, your role, and your strategy for the round. A well-coordinated team with smart purchases will always have the advantage over unplanned spending.

Credits are earned through wins, losses, and eliminations. Managing them properly allows your team to stay competitive throughout the match. Sometimes its better to save and plan for a stronger round rather than spending everything at once. Good economy management can often decide the outcome of the game.












RIFLES 🎯

Reliable and powerful at most ranges. Rifles are the most common choice during full-buy rounds.

SMG-s ⚡

Fast and mobile weapons that work best in close-range fights and eco rounds.

Shotguns 💥

Deadly at close range. Perfect for tight corners and aggressive plays.

Snipers 🎯

High damage weapons built for long-range fights and holding angles.

Sidearms 🔫

Used in pistol rounds and when saving credits. Cheap but effective in the right hands.

Tip 💡

Positioning matters just as much as your weapon. Even the strongest gun won’t help if you’re caught in the wrong place.
